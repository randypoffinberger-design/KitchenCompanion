# Kitchen Companion v0.11.0

## Shopping List 2.0 — foundation release

- Groups matching unchecked ingredients under one clean item name.
- Shows each quantity and source recipe on its own line.
- Cleans common preparation wording such as “chopped,” “divided,” and “to taste” from shopping headings.
- Immediately refreshes and highlights items added from Regular Items.
- Adds shopping-item editing and improved store reassignment.
- Adds edit and remove controls for Regular Items.
- Updates shared-list text to preserve grouped quantities and recipe sources.
- Migrates existing shopping rows into the new grouped-entry format without discarding them.
